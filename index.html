Hampta Pass: The Ultimate 4,270m Crossover

Most Himalayan treks show you one landscape, but Hampta Pass offers a dramatic gear shift. Starting from the dense green pine forests and lush meadows of Manali, you trek up to a staggering altitude of 14,100 feet before crossing over into the stark, raw cold desert of the Spiti Valley circuit. It's an intense 5-day adventure best conquered between mid-June and October.

Frequently Asked Questions

Essential trip dynamics explained clear and simple before you board.

Are transport and hotel stays completely included?

Yes. All featured fixed departure itineraries include sanitised end-to-end group transport (ex-Delhi/ex-Dehradun base hubs) along with handpicked boutique stays, premium swiss camps, and standard meal frameworks mentioned in the itinerary catalog.

Can solo travelers join your group trips?

Absolutely! Over 60% of our core travel community comprises solo adventurers. We ensure safety, organize optimized room sharing structures, and foster a healthy, premium community culture where everyone feels right at home.

Are these expeditions friendly for beginners?

Our weekend getaways and cultural circuits (like Jibhi, Manali, and Spiti) are perfectly geared for all age categories. For high-altitude treks like Hampta Pass or Kedarkantha, basic physical endurance is advised, and our expert trek leads assist you all the way.
