Hampta Pass: The Ultimate 4,270m Crossover

Most Himalayan treks show you one landscape, but Hampta Pass offers a dramatic gear shift. Starting from the dense green pine forests and lush meadows of Manali, you trek up to a staggering altitude of 14,100 feet before crossing over into the stark, raw cold desert of the Spiti Valley circuit. It's an intense 5-day adventure best conquered between mid-June and October.

Frequently Asked Questions

Essential trip dynamics explained simple and clear before you board.

Are transport and hotel stays included?

Yes. All scheduled itineraries include end-to-end group transport from our main base hubs along with handpicked hotel stays, home stays, or high-altitude camps as outlined clearly in the shared day-wise plan catalog.

Can solo travelers join your group trips?

Absolutely. Over 60% of our travel community consists of solo adventurers. We manage clean room sharing options split comfortably by gender and build a social environment right from day one.

Are these trips suitable for absolute beginners?

Our weekend packages and main valley circuits are perfectly designed for anyone. For high-altitude treks, basic stamina helps, and our experienced trip captains walk with you step-by-step.
